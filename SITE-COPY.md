## 6. Process Section

**Section Label:** How We Work

**Section Title:** From Blueprint to Loading Dock

**Steps (3):**

**01 — Analysis**
We study your product dimensions, weight distribution, shipping method, and storage conditions. Every pallet starts with understanding the problem it needs to solve.

**02 — Design**
Using our Computer-Aided Pallet Design System, we engineer the optimal configuration — balancing structural integrity, material efficiency, and cost.

**03 — Manufacturing**
Precision-cut, assembled, and quality-checked under one roof. Heat-treated and stamped if required. Out the door same-day if you need it.

- **Sentence length:** Short. Declarative. Occasionally fragmented for rhythm.
- **Numbers:** Always concrete. "30+ years" not "decades of experience." "24/7" not "always available."
- **Brand position:** We're not a vendor. We're the infrastructure behind your infrastructure.
