About Us

One of the largest manufacturers in the GTA

Our Heritage

Three Decades of Building Better

Founded in the late 1990s, S&B Pallets Ltd. began with a straightforward mission: build better pallets, deliver them faster, and never compromise on quality. What started as a small operation in Scarborough has grown into one of the largest soft and hardwood pallet manufacturers in the Greater Toronto Area.

Our dedication to quality runs through everything we do — from sourcing premium lumber to precision manufacturing with our computer-aided Pallet Design System. We operate our own transport fleet, S&B Transport, ensuring same-day delivery across the GTA with 24/7 availability, 365 days a year.

As an ISPM-15 certified facility and proud member of both the CWPCA and NWPCA, we meet and exceed international standards for pallet manufacturing. Every pallet that leaves our facility is production-checked for consistency, durability, and compliance — because your supply chain depends on it.
